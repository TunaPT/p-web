Through the alleys of the city,

Amidst the fog and the night,

London is majestic,

With its history and tradition bright.

From the Tower of London to Big Ben,

From Notting Hill to Camden Town,

The city is a cultural mosaic,

Where the old and the new come down.

The Thames flows strong and bold,

While the wind blows cold and free,

London is unique and beautiful,

A treasure for all the world to see.
